Question 10: Does bmi of males differ significantly from that of females¶

Perform hypothesis test: independent two sample t-test¶

1. Choose 30 samples each ranmomly from male and female population for bmi¶

2. Define hypothesis¶

H0: two populations mean for bmi does not differ¶

H1: the two population means are different¶

This is two sample t-test¶

3. Do the two sample t-test on the sample data¶

4. Compare p-value with 0.05 for 95 % confidence¶

5. Reach one of the following conclusion: Reject the original position in favor of alternate position or fail to reject the initial position.¶

Following test shows that bmi of males and female does not differ significantly¶
